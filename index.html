// Publications & Research

Peer-reviewed work at
the frontier

Published · Management World, Sep 2022
Smart Courts & Trade Credit Financing
One of the earliest empirical studies linking judicial digitization to corporate financial behavior — showing how "Smart Court" systems changed how businesses extend and access trade credit. Relevant to legal tech, fintech lending, and government modernization.
Published · Venture Capital, Oct 2022
The Dark Side of Initial Coin Offerings
Empirical investigation of corporate misconduct in ICO fundraising markets — providing rigorous evidence on governance and fraud risks in crypto at a time when U.S. regulators were still formulating their response.
SSRN Working Paper · University of Miami
How Firms Respond to Cybersecurity Attacks Through Lobbying
Examined corporate political strategy after data breaches — finding that firms ramp up Washington lobbying as a non-market response to cyber incidents. Novel findings for AI governance, data privacy policy, and enterprise risk management.
SSRN Working Paper · University of Miami
Innovation Responses to Cartel Formation
Analyzed how non-cartel competitors respond strategically to cartel formation through increased innovation — bridging antitrust economics and corporate strategy with implications for U.S. competition policy and tech market regulation.
Published · Advances in Computer Science Research, Nov 2024
Smart Manufacturing & Productivity Growth
Quantified the real productivity gains from smart manufacturing adoption across listed firms — one of the first large-sample causal estimates of industrial AI's economic returns. Relevant to U.S. manufacturing competitiveness and industrial policy.
Published · Data Management Proceedings, Nov 2024
Public Data Governance: Policy Comparison & Practice
Cross-national comparison of public data governance frameworks — covering the EU, U.S., and Asia — with practical recommendations for data marketplace design, CBDC policy, and open government data initiatives.
